Vanilla solitaire

This is a vanilla solitaire game. Move cards up to the ace stacks in ascending
order and same suit, ace first. Move cards from one row to another so that
cards are in alternating color and descending order one by one. You can move
kings to empty rows. If a card is free you can turn it upside. If you can't
move you can draw cards from the deck. If you empty the deck three times
